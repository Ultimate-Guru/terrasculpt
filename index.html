About TerraSculpt

Excellence Since 2016

TerraSculpt is a full-fledged design/build landscape and urban design studio founded in 2016 with a focus to create world-class landscapes centered on innovative practices in landscape and urban design. We strive to enhance the potential of every project site through client collaboration, thorough site analysis, conceptualization through to final site design.
